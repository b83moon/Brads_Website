Projects

Battery Management System

A system the redistributes energy from the stronger cells to the weaker cells in a battery pack in order to maximize the capacity avaiable to the load at its operating voltage.

  • Learn More

Plasma Speaker

A loudspeaker which varies air pressure via a high-energy electrical plasma. Just plug in your phone, turn on some good tunes and voila! Music!

  • Learn More

Hamster Home

Hamster Home tracks the wellbeing of your hamster. It monitors water levels, tracks the distance the hamster has ran and allow you to feed the hamster from the website. This project was a 2016 Hack the North winner!

  • Learn More

Modern Weasley Clock

Based on the Harry Potter Weasley Clock, the hardware and iOS app that lets you know where your family or roommates are in the locations that matter. This project was a 2017 Hack the North winner!

  • Learn More

Modern Weasley Clock V 2.0

Since we received $1000 from 1517 at Hack the North for the Modern Weasley Clock, we decided to continue working on the project and start version 2.0.

  • Learn More

Laser Harp

A musical instrument thats puts a spin on a harp by replacing its strings with lasers. It plays a corresponding note when the user touches one of the laser beams.

  • Learn More

PCB Heart

A simple heart PCB that slowly blinks LEDs when turned on. It was a fun project that I made for Michelle :)
