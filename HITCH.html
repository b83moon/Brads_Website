HITCH

Intelligently delivers affordable and relevant educational videos in emerging markets.

What We're Building

HITCH creates video hotspots that store educational videos offline, for students to watch. Students connect to the HITCH hotspot and can browse, stream and download thousands of videos, over WiFi. This gives schools a competitive edge by offering affordable access to enhanced learning resources like world-class educational video content. HITCH is a small team with ambitious goals, our vision is to be the go-to platform for all educational video content delivery in emerging markets, starting with private schools.
