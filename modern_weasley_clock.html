Modern Weasley Clock

Based on the Harry Potter Weasley Clock, the hardware and iOS app that lets you know where your family or roommates are in the locations that matter. This project was a 2017 Hack the North winner!

Description

The goal of this system is to prolong the life time of battery packs. Over time battery packs need to be replaced as individual cells begin to degrade. Our system is designed to transfer energy from the battery pack to any weak cell. The picture to the left shows the dissipation of 4 cells. Over time in a typical battery pack, cells will significantly degrade and its voltage will drop off sooner than expected. These weak cell(s) act as a “weak point” and reduces the ability of the pack to hold a charge. Once the voltage of weak cell drop, the entire pack voltage will no longer be sufficiently high enough to power the load even if the rest of the cells still output the correct voltage. The purpose of our BMS is to transfer charge from the entire pack to the “weak” cell before the voltage drops below the minimum operating point.
